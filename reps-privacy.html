Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Reps Intervals ("the App"), developed by Haponiuk ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Reps Intervals is designed to work 100% offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Workout presets and configurations you create
  • Workout history and session logs
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

2. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob — We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Firebase — We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage patterns and detect crashes. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data and crash reports.
Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Play Billing — If you purchase the "Remove Ads" option, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.
Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • VIBRATE — To provide haptic feedback during workout intervals
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To restore scheduled workout reminders after device restart
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — To deliver precise workout reminder notifications at the time you configure
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — To keep the timer running when the screen is off during a workout
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To send workout reminder notifications (Android 13+)
  • INTERNET — Required by AdMob to serve advertisements

4. Data Retention

All workout data stored by the App resides exclusively on your device. You can delete all App data at any time through your device's Settings → Apps → Reps Intervals → Clear Data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete on our end. For data stored locally on your device, you have full control through your device settings.
